What is it?

The Bonk Leagues Client is a modified client for the game bonk.io.

Bonk Leagues Client is community-run and offers many unique features that bonk.io itself does not.


Features
  • Bonk Leagues Client will include many useful and helpful features, such as custom servers, enhanced gameplay, and more community features.
